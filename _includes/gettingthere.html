Getting There

Parking at the hotel is limited, so we kindly request that as many guests as possible who are travelling from the Peak District area use the coaches that we have arranged.

The coaches will be provided by Lathkill Coaches and will leave from The Old Bowling Green, Winster.

For guests who will be joining us for the whole day, the coach will depart at 2pm. For evening guests, the coach will depart at 8pm. A returning coach will leave the venue at 12.15am to bring you all back to Winster. There will be a £15 charge per person for the coach.

For those travelling by car, follow your SatNav to:

Losehill House & Spa
Lose Hill Lane
Edale Road Derbyshire S33 6AF
