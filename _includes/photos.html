Photos

We're using a private photo sharing web-site called Cluster to share photos of our wedding. You can upload your photos directly from a smart phone using the Cluster App or using the web-site.

You will need to create an account on Cluster in order to view or share photos from this group but sign-up is easy and free.

If you haven't received an invite to the group by e-mail and would like to view or contribute then please click here, create an account and request to join.
