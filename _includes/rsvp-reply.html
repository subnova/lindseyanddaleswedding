Accommodation

All the rooms at the hotel are exclusively available to The Wedding Party and Guests. We have pre-allocated rooms to those who are travelling from outside the district. Payment for the rooms will be direct to the hotel at checkout. Please let us know whether you would like to take up any allocated rooms, or be added to the waiting list for any rooms that become available.

Transport

Coaches have been booked between the Old Bowling Green, Winster and the hotel to bring both day and evening guests from the area to Losehill House and take everyone home at the end of the night. Please let us know if you would like a place on the coaches so we can get the right sized coach. A volunteer on the coach home will collect the cash and pay the driver. If you're on the waiting list for accommodation we will take you off the transport list if we're able to allocate you a place.
